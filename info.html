Weather App Guide

This is a dynamic webpage.

  • The background of the webpage changes according to weather of the given city.
    It has following background presets,

    1. Clouds : For clear or partly cloudy weather.

    2. Haze : For foggy weather.

    3. Rainy : For moderate to heavy rain.

    4. Thunder : For storms.

    5. Snow : For regions with snowfall.

  • The icons near the weather data also change dynamically.

  • Also there is even a unique note written at the end of the page according to the type of weather
